Welcome to Kinshasa

Kinshasa, the vibrant capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo, is full of culture, history, and natural beauty.

Congo River

The Congo River is one of the most powerful rivers in the world and flows through Kinshasa, shaping its life and culture.

National Museum of Congo

The National Museum of Congo showcases the rich heritage and traditions of the Congolese people.

My Valley Lake

My Valley Lake, located near Kinshasa, is a peaceful retreat surrounded by lush greenery.

Martyrs Stadium

Martyrs Stadium is a major sports venue in Kinshasa, hosting football matches and large events.
